About

Who We Are

Comprised of various high school students and college students across the city, we love building things and sharing our love with others.

Where We're Located

Meeting all across the city, we partner with New York City's Parks Department to build our awesome tinkering community.

What We Do

Creating awesome projects, going on hacker trips to cool tech companies, and networking with other creatives we reach out to everyone in the city.
